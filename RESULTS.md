## Notes (data-derived)

- `compound` preview is 1.16× `simple` (rpow over wad-scaled `(1+r/n)^days`).
- `floating` adds one external CALL to the rate oracle: +5,454 gas vs `simple`.
- `kpi-linked` adds one external CALL to the KPI oracle: +5,479 gas vs `simple`.
- `tiered` (2 bands) adds +6,852 gas vs `simple`. Cost scales linearly with band count.
- `two-track` previewAccrual returns hard-interest portion only: +161 gas vs `simple` (one extra multiply by hardPortionBps).
- `postInterest` cost is dominated by the inner accrual call + storage writes to `accruedInterest` and `principal`.
- Strategy deployment cost ranges 371,686 – 534,152 gas. Constructors with oracles, tiers, or two-track portions cost more.
- `Deploy deposit` (factory creating an InterestBearingDeposit instance) is essentially flat at ~572,201 gas regardless of strategy.
