## Notes (data-derived)

- `compound` preview is 1.16× `simple` (rpow over wad-scaled `(1+r/n)^days`).
- `floating` adds +5,454 gas vs `simple` (one external CALL to rate oracle).
- `kpi-linked` adds +5,479 gas vs `simple` (one external CALL to KPI oracle).
- `tiered` adds +6,863 gas vs `simple` (2 bands; scales linearly).
- `two-track` adds +91 gas vs `simple` (one extra multiply by hardPortionBps).
- `postInterest` cost = inner accrual + storage writes to `accruedInterest` and `principal`.
- Strategy deployment ranges 371,686 – 534,608 gas.
- `Deploy deposit` (factory call) ~672,925 gas regardless of strategy.
